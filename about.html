Hello! I'm Jovana. Come visit my world.

I hold a Bachelor's in English Literature from the University of Chicago. I graduated with general honors. I am currently an MFA student at Parson's at the New School studying Design and Technology. I am still working out exactly my focus, but currently my areas of interest are: design research, accessibility and sustainability in design, data visualization and storytelling, urban landscapes, and collapsing analog products with digital tools.

Ultimately, I would like to design beautiful and accessible products through digital and nondigital tools that inspire and reinforce creativity, and leave meaningful impacts on people and the world.

Coding has been a journey of creativity and problem-solving for me. I see it not just as a technical skill, but as a medium for storytelling, art, and innovation.
