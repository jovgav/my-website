Exquisite Corpse

In this assignment, we were paired up in groups of 3 or 4, and each member of the group had to draw a sketch containing 3 parts: head, torso, and legs. Then, we randomly were given one section of this sketch from each member of the group. We then implemented parts of these sketches into a completed 'Exquisite Corpse'. In my rendering, I leaned into the body horror aspect of the assignment a la Cronengberg. Since one of my classmates had a sketch based on a mushroom pasta dish he had the night before, I decided to make my sketch food themed, and thus Meatball Head was born! I also made this an interactive sketch, with a spoon that you use to feed the meatball. I really took the quirkiness of the assignment to its full extent. I am including sketches from my other members, so you can see from where I pulled to come up with my final rendering!
