Autobiographical Game

With the autobiographical game assignment, I really wanted to focus on narrative as the main "medium" of the game. It is broken up into three sections that reflect three distinct phases of my adult life: college in Chicago, the first few years of post-grad living in LA, and moving back to NYC and starting graduate school at Parsons. In the first phase of the games, the word searches reflect my academic life as an English major. The maze in the second phase represents hiking through the forest in California. The final phase is a latte art cup. wherein you can design whatever you'd like, reflecting my job at a local coffee shop. Together. these game elements reflect my journey so far in life.
