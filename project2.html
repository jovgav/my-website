Face Generator

This prompt was to create an interactive face. As a new coder, I wanted to keep it simple, and so I animated my face by having the eyes and lips change size depending on where the cursor was, and the heart to change color depending on clicking the sketch. I challenged myself here by attempting to create a heart in p5, difficult in so far as getting the circles and the corners of the triangle to align in such a way that no awkward points stick out. I was very happy with this sketch!
